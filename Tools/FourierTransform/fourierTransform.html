Interactive Fourier Series Visualizer

Enter a custom function and plot its Fourier series

Define f(x), choose the interval [L, U], and keep the number of harmonics you want. The series is displayed in the form a0/2 + Σ an cos(2πn(x-L)/T) + Σ bn sin(2πn(x-L)/T).

Use x, pi, e, sin, cos, tan, abs, sqrt, log, exp, pow, and parentheses. Bounds may also be expressions such as -pi, pi, 2*pi, sqrt(2), etc.
